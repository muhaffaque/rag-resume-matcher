Job Title: Business Analyst
Location: Bengaluru, India
Job Type: Full-time
Experience: 2–5 years
About the Company:
TechNova Solutions is a leading IT services company focused on delivering innovative solutions to drive business growth and operational efficiency.
Job Summary:
We are looking for a detail-oriented Business Analyst (BA) to join our team. The BA will bridge the gap between business stakeholders and the technical team, helping to identify requirements, analyze data, and provide actionable insights for decision-making.
Key Responsibilities:

Gather, document, and analyze business requirements from stakeholders.
Develop detailed functional specifications and use cases.
Create dashboards, reports, and visualizations to support business decisions.
Collaborate with product owners, developers, and QA teams to ensure requirements are implemented accurately.
Perform data analysis to identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities for improvement.
Support project management activities, including prioritization, timelines, and risk assessment.
Participate in workshops, meetings, and presentations with stakeholders to communicate findings.
Required Skills & Qualifications:
Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Finance, Economics, or related field.
2–5 years of experience as a Business Analyst or similar role.
Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.
Proficient in SQL, Excel, Power BI, or Tableau.
Understanding of business processes, KPIs, and metrics.
Excellent communication and documentation skills.
Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies is a plus.
Preferred Qualifications:
Experience in IT services or software industry.
Basic knowledge of programming or scripting (Python, R) for data analysis.
Certification like CBAP, PMI-PBA, or equivalent is an advantage.
Benefits:
Competitive salary and performance bonuses.
Flexible working hours and remote options.
Health insurance, paid leave, and retirement benefits.
Opportunities for training and professional growth.
